About CKiD U25 Equation

The CKiD U25 equation (2025) estimates GFR in children (1-18 years) using serum creatinine, height, age, and race. It is the preferred formula for pediatric renal evaluation.

Normal GFR by age: 1-3 mo ≈ 35–45; 3-12 mo ≈ 45–60; 1–18 y ≈ 90–120 mL/min/1.73 m².

Pediatric Blood Pressure Guidelines

Blood pressure values shown are AHA 2025 norms for age- and height-matched percentiles (50th–99th). Hypertensive cutpoints: ≥ 95th percentile on ≥ 3 separate occasions.

Consider follow-up if consistently ≥ 90th percentile.
